## Ingredients

- `1` cup old-fashioned rolled oats
- `1` cup milk
- `2` egg
- `1` tablespoon unsalted butter
- `1` tablespoon granulated sugar
- `2/3` cup all-purpose flour
- `2` teaspoon baking powder
- `1/4` teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Whisk oats and milk together in a large bowl. Let stand for 10 minutes for the oats to soften. Meanwhile, melt butter and set it aside to cool.
2. Add the butter, eggs, and sugar to the oats and then whisk to combine. Add flour, baking powder, and salt and whisk until just combined; do not overmix. Let stand for 5 minutes more.
3. Preheat griddle to 350Â°F.
4. Pour 1/4 cup batter onto the griddle for each pancake and cook until bubbles appear on the surface and the bottom is golden brown. Flip and let cook until the other side is golden brown.
