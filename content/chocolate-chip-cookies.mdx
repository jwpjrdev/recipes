## Ingredients

- `1 3/4` cup bread flour
- `1/2` teaspoon baking soda
- `14` tablespoon (`1 3/4` stick) unsalted butter
- `1/2` cup granulated sugar
- `3/4` cup dark brown sugar
- `1` tablespoon salt
- `2` tablespoon vanilla extract
- `1` egg and `1` egg yolk
- `1 1/4` cup chocolate chips (semisweet or bittersweet)
- `1` tablespoon molasses

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
2. In a medium bowl, mix bread flour, baking soda, and salt together. Set aside.
3. Add granulated sugar and dark brown sugar into a stand mixer.
4. Brown the butter in a pan and pour into stand mixer. Cream together with the sugars.
5. Add eggs into the stand mixer one at a time.
6. Add vanilla extract and molasses.
7. Add the dry mixture 1 cup at a time.
8. Add chocolate chips.
9. Cover dough with syran wrap and refrigerate for 30-60 minutes and flip over halfway through.
10. Use a small cookie scoop to scoop dough onto a baking sheet, 2 inches apart.
11. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
12. Place on cooling racks until room temperature and enjoy.
