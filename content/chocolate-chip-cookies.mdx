## Ingredients

- `1 3/4` cup bread flour
- `1/2` teaspoon baking soda
- `14` tablespoon (`1 3/4` stick) unsalted butter
- `1/2` cup granulated sugar
- `3/4` cup dark brown sugar
- `1` teaspoon salt
- `2` teaspoon vanilla extract
- `1` egg and `1` egg yolk
- `1 1/4` cup chocolate chips (semisweet or bittersweet)
- `1` tablespoon molasses

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix bread flour, baking soda, and salt together. Set aside.
2. Add granulated sugar and dark brown sugar into a stand mixer.
3. Brown the butter in a pan and pour into stand mixer. Cream together with the sugars.
4. Add eggs into the stand mixer one at a time and then add vanilla extract and molasses.
5. Mix in the dry mixture 1 cup at a time and then add chocolate chips.
6. Cover dough with syran wrap and refrigerate for 30-60 minutes and flip over halfway through.
7. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
8. Use a small cookie scoop to scoop dough onto a baking sheet, 2 inches apart.
9. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
10. Place on cooling racks until room temperature and enjoy.
