---
title: 'Chocolate Pie'
---

## Ingredients

- 1 (9-inch) <a href="/recipes/pie-crust">pie crust</a>, homemade or store bought
- 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
- 3 1/2 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 2 large eggs
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 5 ounce can evaporated milk
- <a href="/recipes/whipped-cream">whipped cream</a>, for serving

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar and cocoa powder. Vigorously whisk in the eggs, butter, and evaporated milk until completely combined and smooth (batter will be thin).
3. Pour batter into the pie crust and bake for about 50-55 minutes or until the filling sets and there is a thin crust on the top.

<Tip>
It might be slightly jiggly in the center but as it sits and cools, it will set.
</Tip>

4. Remove from oven and let cool completely. This can take multiple hours.
5. Slice and serve with <a href="/recipes/whipped-cream">whipped cream</a> or chocolate shavings.
