---
title: 'Snickerdoodle Cookies'
---

## Ingredients

### Snickerdoodle Dough

- 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup unsalted butter, slightly softened
- 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

### Cinnamon Sugar Coating

- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â° F.
2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt together. Set aside.
3. In a stand mixer, cream together slightly softened butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla and blend well.
4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix well.
5. In a small bowl, combine remaining 1/3 cup granulated sugar and 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon.

<Tip>
You can chill the dough for thicker cookies with less cracks.
</Tip>

6. Use a small cookie scoop or spoon to scoop out dough, roll into a ball, and then roll in the cinnamon-sugar mixture twice.
7. Place 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.

<Tip>
You can slightly flatten the dough balls for flatter, chewier cookies.
</Tip>

8. Bake for 8-10 minutes. Let sit on the cookie sheet for a few additional minutes before moving to a wire rack to cool.
