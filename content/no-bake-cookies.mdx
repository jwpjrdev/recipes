---
title: 'No-Bake Cookies'
category: 'cookies'
---

## Ingredients

- `2` cup granulated sugar
- `8` tablespoon (`1` stick) unsalted butter
- `1/2` cup milk
- `3` cup quick cooking oats
- `3` tablespoon cocoa powder
- `1` teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Place sugar, butter, and milk in a medium saucepan. Bring to a rolling boil over medium-low heat; boil for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
2. Remove from heat and mix in oats, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper. Let cool until hardened or place in refridgerator.
