## Ingredients

- `16` tablespoon (`2` stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled until no longer warm to the touch
- `2` cup (`400`g) granulated sugar
- `2` tablespoon milk
- `2` egg
- `1/2` teaspoon vanilla extract, optional
- `2 3/4` cup (`345`g) all-purpose flour
- `1` teaspoon baking powder
- `1/2` teaspoon cream of tartar
- `3/4` teaspoon salt

### Topping

- `3` tablespoon granulated sugar
- `1 1/2` teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Prepare a 13x9 pan by lining it with parchment paper and then spraying it with nonstick spray.
2. In a large bowl, combine melted butter, sugar, and milk. Stir well.
3. Add in eggs and vanilla extract and stir until combined.
4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, cream of tartar, and salt.
5. Stir dry ingredients into butter mixture until combined.
6. Spread batter evenly into prepared 13x9 pan and prepare topping.

### Topping

1. In a small bowl, whisk together granulated sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle evenly over snickerdoodle blondie batter.
2. Bake for 25-28 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean or with moist crumbs (not wet batter).
